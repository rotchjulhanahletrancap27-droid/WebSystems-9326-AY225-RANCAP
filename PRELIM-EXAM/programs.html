Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Build a strong foundation in algorithms, programming, and computational theory

Duration: 4 years
Units: 165 units
Degree: Bachelor of Science

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of computing fundamentals, advanced programming techniques, and theoretical computer science. This program prepares graduates for careers in software development, systems analysis, research, and various technology-driven fields.

Students will develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills while learning to design, implement, and maintain complex software systems. The curriculum emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical applications, ensuring graduates are well-equipped to adapt to the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Core Competencies

  • Advanced programming and software development
  • Data structures and algorithms design
  • Database management and design
  • Computer architecture and operating systems
  • Software engineering principles and practices
  • Web and mobile application development
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning basics
  • Computer networks and security fundamentals

Career Opportunities

  • Software Engineer / Developer
  • Systems Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • Full Stack Developer
  • Research Scientist
  • Technical Consultant
  • Quality Assurance Engineer
  • Solutions Architect

Curriculum Highlights

The program spans four years with carefully structured courses that progress from fundamentals to advanced topics. Students engage in hands-on laboratory work, team projects, and internship opportunities with industry partners.

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Passing score on entrance exam
  • Mathematics proficiency
  • Interview with program director

Key Features

  • Industry partnerships
  • Modern computer laboratories
  • Research opportunities
  • Internship programs
  • International certifications

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Master practical IT skills and enterprise system management

Duration: 4 years
Units: 162 units
Degree: Bachelor of Science

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology focuses on the practical application of technology in business and organizational settings. Students learn to design, implement, and manage information systems that support business operations and strategic objectives.

This program emphasizes hands-on experience with current technologies, tools, and methodologies used in the IT industry. Graduates are prepared to work as IT professionals who can bridge the gap between technical solutions and business needs.

Core Competencies

  • Network administration and management
  • System integration and deployment
  • Database design and administration
  • Web technologies and development
  • IT project management
  • Cloud computing and virtualization
  • IT security and risk management
  • Business process analysis

Career Opportunities

  • IT Manager
  • Network Administrator
  • Systems Administrator
  • Database Administrator
  • Web Developer
  • IT Consultant
  • Technical Support Specialist
  • Cloud Solutions Engineer

Industry Certifications

Students are prepared for various industry certifications including CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, Cisco CCNA, Microsoft certifications, and AWS certifications, enhancing their employability and professional credentials.

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Passing score on entrance exam
  • Basic computer literacy
  • Personal statement

Program Strengths

  • Industry-aligned curriculum
  • Certification preparation
  • Hands-on laboratories
  • Business IT focus
  • Internship placement

Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity

Protect digital assets and combat cyber threats

Duration: 4 years
Units: 165 units
Degree: Bachelor of Science

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity addresses the critical need for professionals who can protect organizations from cyber threats. This program provides comprehensive training in security principles, ethical hacking, digital forensics, and risk management.

Students learn both offensive and defensive security techniques, gaining hands-on experience in secure system design, penetration testing, and incident response. The program emphasizes ethical considerations and legal frameworks surrounding cybersecurity.

Core Competencies

  • Network security and defense
  • Ethical hacking and penetration testing
  • Cryptography and encryption
  • Digital forensics and incident response
  • Security risk assessment and management
  • Secure software development
  • Cloud security
  • Compliance and security frameworks

Career Opportunities

  • Cybersecurity Analyst
  • Ethical Hacker / Penetration Tester
  • Security Consultant
  • Digital Forensics Specialist
  • Security Operations Center Analyst
  • Chief Information Security Officer
  • Incident Response Specialist
  • Security Architect

Certifications Pathway

The program prepares students for industry-leading certifications including CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker), CompTIA Security+, CISSP, and CISM, providing pathways to advanced security roles and specialized career opportunities.

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Entrance exam with logic component
  • Background check required
  • Ethics statement agreement

Special Features

  • Cyber range training environment
  • Industry partnerships
  • Capture the Flag competitions
  • Security clearance guidance
  • Internships with security firms
