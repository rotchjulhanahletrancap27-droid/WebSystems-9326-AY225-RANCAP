About Our College

Building tomorrow's technology leaders since 1985

Who We Are

The College of Computer Studies is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in computing education and research. For over three decades, we have been at the forefront of technological innovation, preparing students to become leaders in the rapidly evolving world of technology.

Our college brings together distinguished faculty, cutting-edge facilities, and a dynamic curriculum that blends theoretical foundations with hands-on practical experience. We pride ourselves on creating an environment where creativity, critical thinking, and technical excellence converge.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class computer science and information technology education that empowers students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical foundation needed to excel in the global technology landscape. We are committed to fostering innovation, research excellence, and industry partnerships that create meaningful impact on society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading institution in computing education and research, known for producing graduates who drive technological innovation, demonstrate exemplary leadership, and contribute to solving complex global challenges through the responsible application of technology.

Our Journey

College History

1985

Foundation

The College of Computer Studies was established with just 50 students and 5 faculty members, offering a single BS Computer Science program.

1992

Expansion

Introduction of the BS Information Technology program. Student enrollment reaches 500. Construction of dedicated computer laboratories begins.

2005

Recognition

CCS receives accreditation from the Philippine Association of Colleges and Universities Commission on Accreditation (PACUCOA). Establishes research centers for AI and Cybersecurity.

2015

Innovation Hub

Launch of the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Center. Partnership programs established with major tech companies including Microsoft, Google, and IBM.

2020

Digital Transformation

Successfully transitions to hybrid learning model. Introduces new degree programs in Data Science and Cybersecurity. Student population exceeds 1,200.

2025

Leading the Future

Opening of the state-of-the-art AI Research Laboratory. Launch of BS Artificial Intelligence program. Celebrating 40 years of excellence in computing education.
